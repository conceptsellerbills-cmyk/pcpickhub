## Motor Performance: 110,000 RPM

The headline specification of the MegaFlow 50 is its **110,000 RPM brushless motor** — and unlike many air duster claims that inflate RPM numbers, this figure translates into genuinely felt airflow that dislodges compacted dust from heatsinks, fan blades, and between keycaps with ease.

The brushless motor design is critical here. Brushed motors lose efficiency over time as their contact points wear down. Brushless motors have no mechanical contact between moving parts, which means:

- **Longer lifespan** — rated for thousands of hours of operation without degradation
- **Higher efficiency** — more of the battery energy converts to airflow rather than heat
- **Less vibration** — quieter at equivalent RPM compared to brushed alternatives
- **Consistent performance** — no power drop as the motor ages

At 110,000 RPM, the WOLFBOX MegaFlow 50 generates airflow strong enough to visibly deflect fan blades in an open PC case, remove debris from mechanical switch housings on keyboards, and clear fine dust from speaker grilles and camera lenses without touching the surface.

Mechanical keyboards are dust and crumb traps. Between keycaps, under stabilizers, inside switch housings — debris accumulates constantly. The MegaFlow 50 dislodges it all without requiring keycap removal. A 30-second pass at medium speed visibly clears the spaces between switches that make keyboards feel gritty and unresponsive.

Laptop thermal throttling is often caused by dust-clogged vents. The focused straight nozzle on the MegaFlow 50 directs airflow precisely into laptop exhaust and intake vents, clearing blockages without opening the chassis. This is particularly valuable for thin-and-light laptops where the vents are narrow and canned air can't generate enough sustained pressure.

### Car Interior Detailing
Air conditioning vents, door pocket seams, dashboard crevices, center console gaps — car interiors have dozens of dust traps that detailing brushes and vacuums can't fully reach. The MegaFlow 50's bent nozzle accesses these areas directly, blowing debris loose for easier vacuuming afterward.

### Audio & Photography Equipment
Speaker grilles, microphone capsules, camera body mirror chambers, lens mount contacts — all benefit from gentle, controlled airflow. Speed 1 on the MegaFlow 50 provides exactly the right pressure for these sensitive applications without risking damage from excessive force.

### Outdoor & Workshop Use
Garden tools, outdoor furniture, workshop benches, and power tool vents all accumulate debris that a portable air duster handles faster than any brush or vacuum. The cordless design means no cable management in the workshop or on the patio.
