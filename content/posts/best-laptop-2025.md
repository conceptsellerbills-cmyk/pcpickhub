# Best Laptops 2025: Top 12 for Every Use Case and Budget

## Laptop Buying Guide 2025

**RAM:** 16GB minimum for most workflows in 2025. 8GB is acceptable for light use but feels constrained with multiple browser tabs + apps. 32GB for professional/creative work.

**Storage:** 512GB minimum. 1TB recommended. NVMe SSD is standard and essential — avoid HDD laptops.

**Display:** OLED is worth paying for if you work with visuals. For everyday use, a quality IPS panel is fine.

**Battery:** Advertised battery life is always optimistic. Real-world is typically 60-75% of the spec. MacBooks consistently outperform their spec; Windows laptops often underperform.

**Weight:** Under 3 lbs for portability-first. 3-4 lbs is the sweet spot for most. Gaming laptops typically 5-7 lbs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Mac vs Windows in 2025?
For battery life and performance-per-watt: Mac wins. For gaming: Windows wins (MPS drivers are improving but not there yet). For software compatibility: depends on your specific tools.

### Is 8GB RAM enough in 2025?
For light use (web, Office, video streaming): yes. For developers, video editors, or power users: no. 16GB is the new minimum.

### Should I buy now or wait?
Intel Lunar Lake and AMD Strix Point laptops are available in 2025 with significant efficiency improvements. If your current laptop is functional, waiting 6 months for mid-2025 models makes sense.
